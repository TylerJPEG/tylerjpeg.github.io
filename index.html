About me

I'm Tyler JPEG, an innovative and performance-driven Product Designer, with a background in advertising. I began my career as a Graphic Designer in online advertising, working my way up to Creative Director of an advertising service and technology company. It became apparent that I could transition many of my skills and visual eye to the world of product design. Today, I have 5+ years experience designing mobile and web-based products. As a hands-on Product Designer, I have served many roles and have a broad experience base which includes: front-end development, user interface design, user experience, product development, team management, visual identity, branding and marketing.
